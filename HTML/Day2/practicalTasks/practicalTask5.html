Whispers of the Dawn

The night fades slow, the stars take flight,
As golden beams embrace the night.
A breath of hope, so soft, so near,
The world awakes — the sky is clear.

The winds hum songs of dreams reborn,
Through fields of light and waves of morn.
Each heart now beats with gentle grace,
Beneath the sun’s warm, bright embrace.
— Written by Surendhiran Ravichandran
Karur, India
