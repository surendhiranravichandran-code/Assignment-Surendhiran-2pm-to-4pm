Sony PlayStation 5 (PS5)

The Sony PlayStation 5 is a next-generation gaming console that delivers ultra-fast performance, stunning 4K graphics, and a truly immersive gaming experience. Powered by a custom AMD CPU and GPU, the PS5 brings lifelike visuals and lightning-speed gameplay to your living room.

With the revolutionary DualSense wireless controller, you’ll feel every action through adaptive triggers and haptic feedback, making every moment more realistic than ever before. The PS5 also supports ray tracing, HDR, and 3D audio technology for a cinematic experience.

Key Features:

  • Processor: Custom AMD Ryzen Zen 2 CPU
  • Graphics: AMD RDNA 2 GPU with ray tracing
  • Storage: 825GB ultra-fast SSD
  • Resolution: Up to 8K output / 4K gaming at 120 FPS
  • Audio: Tempest 3D AudioTech for immersive sound
  • Controller: DualSense with adaptive triggers and haptic feedback
