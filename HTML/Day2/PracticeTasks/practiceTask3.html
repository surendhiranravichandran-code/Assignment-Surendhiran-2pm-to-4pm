Poem

The night feels long and cold,
And dreams seem faint, not bold,
Remember — stars can’t shine so bright,
Without the curtain of the night.

Each step you take, though small, unsure,
Is shaping paths that will endure.
Mountains bow to those who climb,
One breath, one hope, one rise at a time.

So lift your heart, don’t count the scars,
You’re made of strength, like silent stars.
The storm will pass, the dawn will glow —
Keep going on — just trust and grow.
